# AI-Travel-Planning-Agent-Travel-Mate-
An AI-powered travel planning application that helps users plan trips with real-time weather updates, tourist attractions, and personalized recommendations. It offers smart trip management and location-based services to make travel planning easy and efficient.

### My Trips
Travel Mate allows users to store their travel history. They can add their Travel Mate friends to trip and share their history with others. They always have option to keep their trips private.
